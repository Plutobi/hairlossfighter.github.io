You Might Be Wondering...

Is this safe to use while I'm breastfeeding? +
Absolutely. Every recipe in this guide uses natural, food-grade ingredients. Virgin coconut oil is so safe that many lactation consultants recommend it as a nipple cream. There are no synthetic chemicals anywhere in this guide.
Will my hair actually grow back? +
For the vast majority of postpartum women, yes — completely. Postpartum hair loss is almost always temporary. The methods in this guide don't just wait for recovery to happen passively — they actively support and speed up your body's natural regrowth process.
I have 4C / coily / Afro-textured hair. Is this guide for me? +
Yes — there is an entire dedicated chapter (Chapter 6) written specifically for natural, coily, and Afro-textured hair. It covers the LOC method, low porosity tips, protective styling scalp care, and a specific edge restoration routine.
I'm exhausted. Is this realistic to follow with a baby? +
This guide was written with you in mind. The 30-day plan is broken into tiny, manageable steps. The minimum effective routine takes about 10 minutes and can be done during nap time. You don't have to do everything — just start with one thing.
How quickly will I see results? +
Most mamas notice reduced shedding and a healthier scalp within 2 to 4 weeks. Visible baby hairs at the hairline typically appear within 4 to 8 weeks of consistent use. Full density recovery takes 3 to 6 months — but every week of consistent care makes a difference.
